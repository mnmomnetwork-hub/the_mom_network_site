Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about The Mom Network

Community members donate weekly (suggested $2, but any amount is welcome). All donations are pooled together and given to one selected family each week to help with their immediate needs.

Any parent or caregiver in the United States facing financial hardship can apply. We help families with various challenges including job loss, medical bills, unexpected expenses, and other temporary difficulties.

We review all applications for eligibility and genuine need. Approved applications are then entered into a random selection process each week. This ensures fairness and gives every approved family an equal chance.

The amount varies based on community participation that week. It could range from a few hundred to several thousand dollars, depending on how many people donate and the amounts given.

Currently, The Mom Network is not a registered 501(c)(3) organization, so donations are not tax-deductible. We are working toward obtaining this status in the future.

We are committed to transparency. We share updates about weekly winners and how funds are distributed. All donations go directly to families in need - we do not take any administrative fees.

Families can only receive help once through our program. This ensures we can help as many different families as possible. However, you're always welcome to reapply if your circumstances change significantly.

You can help by sharing our mission with others, volunteering your time, sharing your story to inspire others, or helping us spread awareness on social media. Every bit of support helps our community grow.
