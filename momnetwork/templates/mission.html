Our Mission

Building a community of support for families across the United States

What We Believe

We believe that every family deserves support during challenging times. By pooling our resources as a community, we can make a meaningful impact on the lives of parents and caregivers who are struggling to make ends meet.

How We Help

Each week, our community comes together to donate what they can - whether it's $2 or more. These donations are pooled together and given to one selected family to help with their immediate needs such as groceries, utilities, medical bills, or other essential expenses.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we have helped hundreds of families across the United States. Our community-driven approach ensures that help goes directly to those who need it most, creating real change in people's lives.

Join Our Community

Whether you're looking to give back or need support yourself, you're welcome in our community. Together, we can make a difference, one family at a time.
