Who We Help

Supporting parents and caregivers across the United States

New Parents

Parents with newborns facing unexpected medical bills or loss of income during maternity/paternity leave.

Single Parents

Single mothers and fathers working hard to provide for their families while managing all responsibilities alone.

Families Facing Medical Challenges

Parents dealing with their own or their children's medical issues and the financial burden that comes with healthcare.

Families Experiencing Job Loss

Parents who have recently lost employment and are struggling to cover basic needs while searching for new opportunities.

Our Selection Process

We carefully review each application to ensure help goes to families with genuine need. Our selection process considers:

  • Current financial situation and immediate needs
  • Family size and circumstances
  • Temporary vs. long-term challenges
  • Geographic diversity across the United States

Every family's situation is unique, and we strive to help those who would benefit most from our community's support.
